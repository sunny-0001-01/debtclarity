Frequently Asked Questions

How is credit card interest calculated?

Interest is calculated monthly based on your APR. Most payments go toward interest first.

Why does paying minimum take so long?

Minimum payments mostly cover interest, keeping balances high for years.

What is the real cost of a credit card purchase?

It includes your original purchase plus all interest paid over time.

How can I reduce credit card interest?

Increase monthly payments, avoid new charges, and consider balance transfers.
